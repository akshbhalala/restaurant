Our Story

Crafted with Soul,
Served with Grace

Saffron was born from a singular vision — to create a dining experience that transcends the ordinary. Founded in 2012 by Chef Arjun Mehta, our kitchen blends classical French technique with the bold, aromatic spices of the East.

Every dish on our menu is a meditation on balance — where umami meets sweetness, where fire meets delicacy, and where tradition meets innovation. We source only from sustainable farms and artisan producers who share our commitment to quality.

Chef Arjun Mehta

Founder & Executive Chef
